Two agents work in tandem — a research agent scans on-chain data, social signals, and market feeds to surface candidates. A trading agent backtests each one against multiple strategies and only promotes what passes the gate. Briefs flow between them through a structured handoff contract.

Paper mode is the default. Live execution requires explicit approval. Confidence tiers degrade gracefully when data quality drops — from normal operation down to hold-and-observe. Everything is open source and Apache-2.0 licensed. Inspect it all.
